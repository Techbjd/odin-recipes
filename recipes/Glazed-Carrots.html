Glazed Carrots

Its recipe are as follow :

ingredients :

  • 1 pound (450g) baby carrots, or peeled and sliced carrots
  • 1 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) chicken or vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish, optional)

Instructions :

  1. Prepare Carrots:

    2. If you're not using baby carrots, peel and slice carrots into uniform pieces.

  2. Cook Carrots:

    3. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter. Add the carrots and saut√© for about 3-4 minutes until they start to soften.

  3. Prepare Glaze:
    4. Add honey and brown sugar to the carrots. Stir to coat the carrots evenly.

  4. Add Broth:
    5. Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth. Bring the mixture to a simmer.

  5. Simmer:
    6. Reduce the heat to low and let the carrots simmer for about 10-15 minutes or until they are tender. Stir occasionally to ensure even coating.

  6. Season:
    7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the sweetness by adding more honey or sugar if desired.

  7. Serve:
    8. Once the carrots are glazed and tender, transfer them to a serving dish. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley if you like.

  8. Enjoy:
    9. Serve the glazed carrots as a delicious side dish alongside your favorite main course.

  9. Feel free to adjust the sweetness and seasonings according to your taste preferences. Enjoy your Glazed Carrots!
